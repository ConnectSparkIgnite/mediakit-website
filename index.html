About

Why Conference in a Box Exists

In August 2025, during the National Prevention Network conference in Washington, D.C., a group of Prevention Leaders gathered informally. Travel budgets were tightening, states and regions were struggling to bring national presenters in, and a long-standing national prevention conference would soon move to a virtual format.

Conference in a Box was created as a win-win solution.

For States & Regions

Gain access to a slate of national Prevention Leaders at a single, reasonable rate. No more piecing together conferences.

For Prevention Leaders

Gather intentionally, with travel supported and stipends provided. A funded way to convene as a collective.

The Model

What Conference in a Box Is (And Is Not)

What It Is

  • A one-day slate of national Prevention Leaders
  • Built around Five Strategic Pathways
  • Designed, not pieced together
  • Four sessions included
  • Built-in Conversation Break (45 minutes)
  • Customizable experience flow
  • Encouraged as Day 1 of a two-day event

What It Is Not

  • We do not manage logistics
  • We do not handle registration
  • We do not secure venues
  • We do not manage CEUs
  • We are not replacing local leadership

The host manages venue, registration, AV, promotion, CEUs, and coordination.

How It Works

Structure of the Summit

We Provide
  • 4–6 national prevention leaders as presenters
  • Fully designed and curated one-day summit arc
  • Collective Conversation Break featuring Prevention Leaders

The Conversation Break

A 45-minute intentional small-group discussion period where Prevention Leaders serve as topic hosts. This builds connection, demonstrates depth, and reinforces network identity.
